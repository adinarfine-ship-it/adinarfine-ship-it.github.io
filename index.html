Mahjong & Matcha

Privacy Policy

Effective July 13, 2026 · Applies to the “mahjongandmatcha pin publisher” application

Who we are

Mahjong & Matcha is a small independent shop selling mahjong- and matcha-themed goods on Etsy under the shop name mahjongandmatcha. The “mahjongandmatcha pin publisher” is a private tool built and operated solely by the shop owner.

What the application does

The application publishes product pins — images, titles, descriptions, and links to our own Etsy listings — to our own Pinterest profile using the Pinterest API. It is used exclusively by the shop owner on the shop’s own Pinterest account.

Information we collect

None from you. The application:

  • has no public users, sign-ups, or visitors — it cannot collect anything from anyone other than the shop owner;
  • accesses only the shop’s own Pinterest account (its boards and pins) with the owner’s authorization;
  • does not read, collect, store, or process personal information about any other Pinterest user;
  • does not use cookies, analytics, tracking, or advertising technology.

How credentials are handled

Pinterest access tokens for the shop’s account are stored privately on the shop owner’s own computer and are used only to publish and manage the shop’s own pins. They are never shared, sold, or transmitted to any third party other than Pinterest itself.

Sharing and selling of data

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any data with anyone. The only service the application communicates with is the Pinterest API, governed by Pinterest’s own privacy policy.

Data retention and deletion

Because no user data is collected, there is nothing to retain or delete. Pins published to Pinterest can be removed at any time by the shop owner through Pinterest.

Changes to this policy

If the application’s behavior ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this page will be updated and the effective date revised.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to adinarfine@gmail.com or via our Etsy shop.
